Meet Your Captain

Captain Max

Saltwater runs through his veins. Growing up fishing the waters of Westhampton, Captain Max has spent the last 15 years mastering the intricate marine ecosystems that stretch from the Hamptons to Montauk — one of the most productive and storied fishing corridors on the Eastern Seaboard.

Holding a USCG 6-Pack OUPV license, Captain Max combines deep local knowledge with a refined, professional approach. Whether you are chasing trophy Striped Bass at dawn, hunting Bluefin Tuna in the canyons, or entertaining guests aboard your vessel, he delivers a seamless, world-class experience every time. Fully licensed and insured, your safety and satisfaction are always the priority.
